Academic Excellence

UTC offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and graduate programs designed to prepare students for successful careers.

Undergraduate Programs

Choose from more than 140 undergraduate programs across five colleges, with opportunities for research, internships, and study abroad.

Graduate Studies

Advance your career with our master's, doctoral, and certificate programs designed for working professionals and full-time students.

Research Opportunities

Engage in cutting-edge research with faculty mentors in state-of-the-art facilities across all disciplines.

Vibrant Campus Life

At UTC, learning extends beyond the classroom. Our campus offers a rich array of activities, organizations, and resources to enhance your college experience.

  • 200+ student organizations and clubs
  • NCAA Division I athletics with 17 varsity teams
  • State-of-the-art recreation center and facilities
  • Cultural events, concerts, and guest speakers
  • Leadership development and service opportunities
